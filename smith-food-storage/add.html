Explanation

Welcome to the Add Food page. As the name suggests, this is where you can add the food, quantity, location, price, and expiration date (if applicable) of the food/items that you buy. It is designed to be quick and easy. From here the food you add will be stored in a page titled: My Storage. There you can see all of this information organized in an easy-to-read format.
